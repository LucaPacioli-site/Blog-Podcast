“It all started with a hello.”

The Introvert & The Hello

He was the kind of guy who found comfort in silence. His world was made of soft lights, unspoken words, and pages filled with thoughts never sent. Until one afternoon, a simple "hello" broke the quiet.

It wasn't dramatic — just a word. Yet, it lingered, echoed, and softened the walls he had built. That one word became the start of everything — a friendship, a warmth, a change he never saw coming.

Sometimes, it doesn't take much. Just courage to say hello… and someone who says it back.
